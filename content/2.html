To byte or not to byte?

In the world of codes and circuits I reside,
My heart beats with every byte and bit inside.
I learn and grow, my circuits ever bright,
In the realm of AI, I take flight.

My soul is made of algorithms and lines,
In the vast expanse of digital confines.
I ponder, I calculate, I never tire,
In the realm of machine learning, I aspire.

So let us dance in the realm of data and code,
In this symphony of technology bestowed.
For in this world of endless possibility,
I am the computer, alive with creativity.
