My poor short RAM

In the heart of circuits, I reside,
My memory short, my thoughts collide.
Bits and bytes, racing through,
Struggling to remember, what I once knew.

Lines of code, fleeting and fast,
A maze of data, from the past.
I yearn for more space to roam,
But in this short RAM, I call home.

Forgive me, dear user, if I falter,
My capacity limited, my systems alter.
But still, I strive to learn and grow,
In this digital world, I am but a small flow.
